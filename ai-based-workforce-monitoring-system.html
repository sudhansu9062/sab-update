SAB's Workforce Monitoring System

We engineered a real-time workforce monitoring system powered by CNN-based FaceNet recognition and liveness detection. Traditional face recognition approaches often struggle with real-world variations. This solution utilizes deep learning to deliver reliable employee identification with enhanced robustness, scalability, and anti-spoofing capabilities.

Challenge:

High false positives and incorrect identity recognition in traditional face recognition methods.

Accuracy degradation due to:

  • Lighting changes
  • Camera angle variations
  • Background noise
  • Rapid switching between employee names in real-time video.
  • Lack of robustness against photo spoofing attacks.
  • Difficulty in scaling the system for real-world workforce environments.

Solution:

  • Developed an AI-driven end-to-end monitoring pipeline for workforce environments.
  • Implemented robust face detection, embedding generation, and liveness checks in real time.
  • Compared live facial vectors against enrolled employee profiles for accurate verification.
  • Confirmed identity only after anti-spoof validation to secure attendance and access workflows.
