Challenge:

Need for seamless real-time communication between users speaking different Indian languages. Traditional approaches rely on text-based translation or bilingual agents, leading to increased operational costs, latency, and inconsistent customer experience especially in call center environments.

Solution:

Developed an AI-driven, end-to-end voice-to-voice translation pipeline that converts spoken input into translated speech output, enabling continuous turn-by-turn conversations between Hindi and Tamil speakers. The modular design allows easy extension to real-time streaming and additional languages.
